7 Specialized Hair Treatments

Comprehensive trichology care for every type of hair loss, scalp concern, and restoration goal.

PRP & Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC)

Platelet-Rich Plasma and Growth Factor Concentrate therapy stimulates dormant hair follicles using your body's own growth proteins — delivering visible density improvement and reduced hair fall within 2–3 months.

Alopecia Areata Management

Targeted treatment for autoimmune-triggered patchy hair loss. Combines corticosteroid therapy, immunotherapy, and advanced scalp stimulation to restore affected areas and prevent recurrence.

Androgenetic Alopecia Treatment

Medical management of male and female pattern baldness using a combination of FDA-approved topicals, oral treatments, PRP therapy, and personalized protocols to slow progression and stimulate regrowth.

Dandruff & Scalp Psoriasis Treatment

Specialized scalp treatments combining medicated therapies and advanced dermatological protocols to eliminate dandruff, soothe scalp psoriasis, and restore a healthy, flake-free scalp environment.

Common Questions

Hair Treatment FAQs

Typically, 4–6 sessions are recommended, spaced about 4–6 weeks apart. Maintenance sessions may be required every 6–12 months to sustain the results. The exact number depends on your hair loss type, severity, and individual response to treatment.
Results vary by individual, but initial improvements in hair quality and reduction in hair fall are often noticed within 2–3 months. Significant regrowth and density improvement typically occur after 6–8 months of treatment.
Side effects are rare but can include mild redness or swelling at the injection site, and temporary itching or dryness on the scalp. These typically resolve within a few days. Our doctors take every precaution to ensure your comfort and safety throughout the procedure.
With proper maintenance and care, the effects can last 1–2 years or longer. Combining the treatment with a healthy diet, stress management, and prescribed hair care routines significantly extends the benefits. Periodic maintenance sessions help sustain long-term results.
