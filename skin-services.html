Common Questions

Skin Treatment FAQs

The number of sessions depends on your specific skin concern and treatment type. Most skin treatments show noticeable improvement after 2–4 sessions. Dr. Shalini will outline a personalized treatment plan with a clear session schedule during your consultation.
Most treatments involve minimal to no downtime. Procedures like skin tag removal and laser toning allow immediate return to normal activities. Some treatments like chemical peels or fractional lasers may cause mild redness for 1–3 days. Your doctor will provide specific aftercare guidelines.
Yes, all procedures are performed by Dr. Shalini Patodiya using USFDA-approved technologies, ensuring safety and effectiveness. Treatments are specifically tailored for Indian skin types, with protocols designed to minimize risk and maximize results.
Side effects are generally mild and temporary — such as mild redness, swelling, or sensitivity that typically resolves within a day or two. Our dermatologist provides comprehensive aftercare instructions to minimize any discomfort and ensure smooth healing.
Skin lightening treatments target excess melanin production using a combination of laser therapy (Q-switched Nd:YAG, picosecond), chemical peels, and medical-grade topicals containing Vitamin C, niacinamide, and retinoids. These work together to fade dark spots, reduce pigmentation, and reveal a brighter, more even complexion.
Once removed, the same skin tag does not regrow. However, new tags may develop over time in areas prone to friction, especially if you are genetically predisposed. Our doctors also advise on prevention strategies to reduce the likelihood of new tag formation.
Most treatments are performed with topical numbing cream or cooling devices, making them comfortable. Procedures like laser treatments feel like a mild warm sensation, while injections are minimized with fine needles. Dr. Shalini's team prioritizes your comfort throughout every session.
Anyone looking to address skin concerns like acne scars, pigmentation, uneven tone, skin tags, stretch marks, or ageing can benefit. During your consultation, Dr. Shalini will assess your skin type, medical history, and goals to determine the most suitable treatment plan for you.
