Her Approach

Root-Cause Care, Not Quick Fixes

Dr. Shalini Patodiya built Amber Clinics on one principle: every patient's skin deserves a diagnosis, not just a treatment. With 17+ years of practice, she combines clinical depth with cosmetic artistry to address the underlying cause of your concern — whether it's hormonal acne, genetic hair loss, or UV-accelerated aging.

Her background in trichology means hair and scalp conditions are treated with the same rigour as complex skin disorders. Her training in cosmetic gynaecology extends her practice to intimate skin health — an area often underserved in general dermatology.

Amber Clinics uses only USFDA-cleared devices and evidence-backed protocols calibrated for Indian skin tones — ensuring both safety and results that actually hold.

Root-Cause Diagnosis Every consultation starts with understanding why, not just what — so treatments deliver lasting results.
Safe for Indian Skin All protocols are adapted for Fitzpatrick III–VI skin tones. No one-size-fits-all approach.
Personalized Treatment Plans Each patient receives a custom protocol — no off-the-shelf packages or unnecessary procedures.
USFDA-Certified Technology Only cleared, evidence-backed devices and products. Patient safety is always non-negotiable.
