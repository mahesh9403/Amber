Laser Skin Rejuvenation & Anti-Aging
HIFU — High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound

Non-surgical facelift using focused ultrasound energy to target the SMAS layer — stimulating collagen production for visible skin lifting, tightening, and wrinkle reduction. Results last 12–18 months with no downtime.

Carbon Laser Peel (Hollywood Peel)

A celebrity-favourite treatment — carbon lotion is applied to the skin and targeted by laser to deep-cleanse pores, reduce oiliness, improve texture, and deliver an instant glass-skin glow. Zero downtime.

Fractional CO2 Laser

Advanced fractional CO2 laser that creates microscopic skin channels to stimulate intense collagen remodeling — effectively treating deep wrinkles, acne scars, stretch marks, and skin resurfacing.

Laser Tattoo Removal (Q-Switched Nd:YAG)

Laser Toning (Q-Switched Nd:YAG)

Microneedling RF

Laser Treatment FAQs

Yes! Laser hair removal is safe for sensitive areas like the bikini line, Brazilian, and underarms when performed by trained professionals. At Amber Clinics, we use advanced cooling systems and specialized protocols to ensure maximum comfort and safety in sensitive areas.
Laser hair removal provides long-lasting results — most patients see 80–90% permanent hair reduction after a full treatment course. Occasional maintenance sessions may be required over time to address any new hair growth, particularly in hormonal areas like the face.
Results are noticeable after the first few sessions, with significant hair reduction evident after completing your full treatment plan (typically 6–8 sessions). Hair becomes progressively finer and sparser with each session.
Temporary redness, swelling, or sensitivity may occur but typically resolve within a few hours to a day. Our experts provide comprehensive aftercare guidance to minimize risks. All our laser systems are FDA-approved and operated by trained dermatologists.
Yes, HIFU is safe and effective for all skin types and tones. It works by targeting the deeper tissue layers with ultrasound energy, bypassing the surface skin entirely — making it equally effective regardless of skin color or tone.
Many patients see significant improvement after just one HIFU session. Results develop gradually over 2–3 months as collagen rebuilds. Depending on your skin condition and goals, additional sessions may enhance outcomes, with results lasting 12–18 months.
HIFU effectively treats sagging skin on the face, neck, and décolletage, reduces wrinkles and fine lines, provides a non-surgical facelift effect, tightens the jawline, and improves overall skin elasticity and firmness. It can also be used for body tightening on arms, abdomen, and thighs.
Most patients experience mild discomfort — described as a warm, tingling sensation during treatment. This is actually a sign that the treatment is working effectively. For aesthetic applications, no anesthesia is usually required, though numbing cream can be applied if preferred.
